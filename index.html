Our Story

Our History

Founded in 2010 by the Late Archbishop Mvuleni, Anointed in Living Christ started as a small home fellowship with members from different backgrounds. Through God's grace, we have grown into a multi-campus church with branches in Pretoria,Kwazulu-Natal and Mpumalanga, reaching thousands with the gospel.
